Rules of inference

Modus Ponens
    p
    p → q
    ------
    q

Addition
    p
    ---
    p ∨ q

Simplification
    p ∧ q
    ------
    p

Conjunction
    p
    q
    ----
    p ∧ q

Modus Tollens
    ¬q
    p → q
    ------
    ¬p

Hypothetical Syllogism
    p → q
    q → r
    ------
    p → r

Disjunctive Syllogism
    p ∨ q
    ¬p
    -----
    q

Resolution
    p ∨ q
    ¬p ∨ r
    -------
    q ∨ r
